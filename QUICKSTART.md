### Chrome/Edge/Brave
1. Download this project (click the green "Code" button ‚Üí "Download ZIP")

4. Enable "Developer mode" (toggle in top-right)
5. Click "Load unpacked"
6. Select the `visual-history-search` folder
7. Done! ‚úÖ

## First Use (1 minute)

### Step 1: Browse Normally
Just use your browser like normal. The extension automatically captures pages in the background.

**What gets captured:**
- Screenshot of the page
- Page title and URL
- Dominant color
- Whether it has images, videos, or code
- Sample of the text content

**What DOESN'T get captured:**
- Incognito tabs (by default)
- Non-http(s) pages (like chrome:// pages) or (like brave:// pages)
- Anything you exclude in settings

### Step 2: Wait a Bit
Give it a few minutes to capture some pages. Browse 5-10 websites to see it in action.

### Step 3: Search!
1. Click the extension icon (purple in your toolbar)
2. Try searching for something you just visited
3. Or use filters like "Today" or the color picker
4. Click any result to reopen that page

## Common Questions

**Q: How do I know it's working?**
A: After browsing a few sites, click the extension icon. You should see thumbnails of recent pages.

**Q: It's not capturing a page!**
A: Some reasons this happens:
- Page didn't fully load
- It's an incognito tab
- It's a chrome://, brave://, or extension page
- Domain is in your exclusion list

**Q: How much space does it use?**
A: About 100KB per page. So 100 pages ‚âà 10MB. Check Settings ‚Üí Storage to see your usage.

**Q: Can I pause it?**
A: Yes! Settings ‚Üí Toggle "Enable Capture" off. Toggle back on to resume.

**Q: How do I delete everything?**
A: Settings ‚Üí Delete All Data. **This cannot be undone.**

## Troubleshooting

### Extension not capturing pages
1. Check if "Enable Capture" is ON in Settings
2. Make sure you're not in incognito (unless you disabled that exclusion)
3. Try reloading the extension: chrome://extensions ‚Üí Click reload icon

### Search returns no results
1. Give it time - browse a few more pages
2. Try broader search terms
3. Check if auto-cleanup deleted old pages (Settings ‚Üí Storage)

### Performance issues
1. Lower retention period (Settings ‚Üí History Retention)
2. Clean up old entries (Settings ‚Üí Clean Up Old Pages Now)
3. Check your computer's storage isn't full
